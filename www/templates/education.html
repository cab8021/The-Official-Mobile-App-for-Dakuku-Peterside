EDUCATION

Dakuku Peterside was born on December 31, 1970 of Opobo parents. He attended Okrika Grammar School (OGS), Okrika from 1982 to 1987. He played active football in school as school goalkeeper and served as school prefect in 1986/1987 session. Dakuku studied Hematology in the Department of Medical Laboratory Science in Rivers State University of Science and Technology, Port Harcourt. He also earned a post graduate Diploma and Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree from the same university. Dakuku received a Doctorate degree (Ph.D.) in the year 2014 from the University of Port Harcourt in Organizational Behaviour with special interest in corporate political strategy. He is a member of Nigeria Institute of Management (NIM) and a fellow of Institute of management Consultants of Nigeria (IMCN).

He got married to Elima Peterside, a lawyer in October 2000 and they have three children plus three other foster children; Soba, Belema, Miebi, Ibim, Sopirinye and Ibiso.
